Feature branches include:

feat_PersonalInfo
Personal Info - need a form to maintain (add, update & delete) personal information.
Details include: PersonalID, FirstName, LastName, DOB, Gender, Email Address, MobilePhone

feat_ContactDetails
Contact Info - need a form to maintain (add, update & delete) contact details.
Details include: ContactID, FirstName, LastName, CompanyName, MobilePhone

feat_Appointments
Appointment Info - need a form to maintain (add, update & delete) the appointments.
Details include: AppointmentID, EventName, Location, EventDate, StartTime, EndTime

feat_ShoppingList
Shopping List - need a form to maintain the shopping list.
Details include: ShoppingItemID, ShopName, NameOfItem, ShoppingDate, PriceQuoted

Each section will be able to perform these core functions:

  • Create new records
  • Update the existing record
  • Delete the existing records
